# Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
## Key Findings
### Descriptive Statistics
-	The average quantity purchased per order was 2.945833 units.
-	The median quantity purchased per order was 3 units.
-	The average unit price was $356.412750
-	The average Total Price was $1053.968.
### Product Analysis
-	The most frequently purchased product was Printer with 181 orders.
-	The least purchased product was Phone with 156 orders.
-	The product generating the highest revenue was Chair, contributing $195620.11, indicating strong customer demand and making it a key revenue driver for the business.
### Payment Method Analysis
-	The most used payment method was Online, accounting for 21.5% of all transactions.
-	The least used payment method was Gift Card.
### Referral Source Analysis
-	The leading referral source was Instagram, generating 259 customer orders.
-	The referral source contributing the highest revenue was Instagram, generating $275285.45.
### Order Status Analysis
-	19.6% of all orders were successfully shipped.
-	19.8% of orders were pending.
-	20.8% of orders were cancelled.
-	20.6% of orders were Returned.
-	19.2% of orders were Delivered.

## Recommendations
Based on the analysis, the following recommendations are proposed:
-	Increase marketing efforts for Chair, as it generates the highest revenue.
-	Review the performance of Phone and consider promotional campaigns to improve sales.
-	Invest more resources in Credit Card which contributes the highest number of customers/revenue.
-	Monitor pending and cancelled orders to improve fulfillment efficiency.
-	Track unusual purchasing patterns to identify emerging business opportunities.
